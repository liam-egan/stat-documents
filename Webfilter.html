Web filter

The Barrington Public School district uses ContentKeeper to filter Web content and maintain compliance with the Children's Internet Protection Act, or CIPA, a term blocked by ContentKeeper for its inappropriate Urban Dictionary definition.


In Soviet Russia, you no bypass Web filter. Web filter bypass you.

Enabling personal devices to use the Guest network with the Web filter

Personal devices cannot use BPS-Guest without installing a certificate that allows them to interact with ContentKeeper. The certificate is required to enable the decryption of encrypted Web traffic. I cannot explain exactly how this works, since I do not understand how SSL works, but you are welcome to read the Wikipedia article on the matter.


STAT policy is to supervise users while they install the certificate on their personal devices.


  1. Direct the user to http://www.barringtonschools.org/cert.html
  2. ContentKeeper may redirect the browser to a landing page that checks for the certificate. The instructions are the same either way.
  3. Direct the user to download the certificate (available on the linked page above or the ContentKeeper page) and follow the instructions to install the certificate.
  4. Assist them if necessary.
  5. Ensure the Web filter is working properly. Google and The Verge are good test sites, as they are both https:// only.

macOS

The majority of personal devices students bring to the school are DongleBooks, DongleBook Pros, MacBook Airs, or MacBook Pros.


For easy access, here are the instructions for donglemacOS:


  1. Click the 'Root Certificate' button
  2. Double-click the downloaded ckroot.crt from your Downloads folder on your Mac
  3. Enter your Mac's admin username and password if prompted
  4. Choose 'System' from the keychain drop-down list
  5. Double-click the certificate called 'ContentKeeper Root CA'
  6. Expand the 'Trust' section and change 'When using this certificate' to 'Always Trust'
  7. Close the pop-up
  8. Enter your Mac's admin username and password if prompted
  9. Quit the Keychain Application
